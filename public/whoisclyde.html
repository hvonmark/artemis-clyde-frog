The Meow Man

Artemis Clydefrog is our 3-year old bad cat. We got him as a kitten from a rescue after seeing his photo posted on the web. He came chipped, fixed, and bad. But he was a kitten and all kittens are unbelievably cute so we took him home anyway. He had the name Clyde when we got him, so we just expanded it to meet our naming needs.

Bad to the Bone

He walks on countertops, the table, on the bath vanity. He sleeps in the kitchen sink because it's stainless steel and heats up in direct sun. He hangs out on the backs of chairs so he can see what you are eating. He steals turkey legs out of soup bowls and runs downstairs to hide and eat it. He pulls food down from the counter by standing on his back legs and using his claws to reach the edge ofthe plate. We have tried water bottles, time outs, solitary confinement, positive reinforcement -- all to no avail. He is bad.
