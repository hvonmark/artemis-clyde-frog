Why the Ball

It seems cruel at first, but he really likes it. The ball dispenses food through kibble-size holes as Clyde bats it around the floor.

Happiness in a Can

The climax of Clyde's day is the canned cat food we give him for evening feedings. We have to lock him up in another part of the house so he doesn't steal canned cat food from the other cat.
